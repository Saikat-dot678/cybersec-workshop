What is Cyber Shield 2025?

Ever wondered how hackers think? Or how to build a digital fortress to protect against real-world cyber threats?

Cyber Shield 2025 is your chance to dive deep into the thrilling world of cybersecurity. Organized by the CSE Students' Society (CSS) of NIT Durgapur, this intensive 2-day workshop is the ultimate launchpad for 1st and 2nd-year students curious about digital defense. This isn't just another lecture series; it's a hands-on bootcamp designed to take you from zero to hero in cybersecurity fundamentals.

Over 12 hours of immersive sessions, you will:

  • Demystify Cyber Threats: Learn to identify and understand common attacks like Phishing, Malware, Ransomware, and SQL Injection.
  • Get Hands-On with Pro Tools: Step into the shoes of a security professional with practical labs using industry-standard tools like Kali Linux, Wireshark, and Burp Suite.
  • Compete in a Live CTF: Put your newfound skills to the test in a mini Capture The Flag (CTF) competition. Solve puzzles, crack hashes, and hunt for hidden flags in a fun, competitive environment.
  • Build Your Career Roadmap: Gain valuable insights into internships, certifications, and career pathways in the ever-growing field of cybersecurity.

By the end of the workshop, you'll walk away with not just theoretical knowledge, but practical skills, a certificate of participation to boost your resume, and the confidence to navigate the digital world safely.

Whether you're an aspiring ethical hacker or just want to learn how to protect yourself online, Cyber Shield 2025 is your first line of defense. Don't miss out on this opportunity to level up your skills. Secure your spot now!
